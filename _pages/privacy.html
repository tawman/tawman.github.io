Overview

Wood Consulting Partners, LLC respects your privacy. This website (www.woodcp.com) collects no personally identifiable information. We do not ask you to create an account, fill out forms, or provide any personal data to browse the site.

Analytics

We use Umami, a privacy-focused analytics tool, to understand site traffic. Our Umami instance is self-hosted on our own infrastructure — no analytics data leaves systems we control. Umami is cookie-free and collects only anonymous, aggregate metrics such as page views, referrers, and browser type. It does not collect names, email addresses, IP-based identities, or anything that identifies you personally.

No Sharing with Third Parties

We never sell, rent, or share analytics data with third parties. Period.

Google User Data

Applications operated under this domain that offer Google sign-in comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Google account data is used only to provide the features you request, and is never sold, shared for advertising, or used for any other purpose.

Third-Party Links

This site links to external websites we do not control. Their privacy practices are their own; review their policies before providing them any personal information.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, with the effective date updated above.
